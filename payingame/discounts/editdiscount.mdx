---
title: "Edit Discount Codes"
description: "Once a discount is available, you can always edit it later."
---

To edit a discount, go to the "Discount Codes" item in the header and press on "Edit" in the list of discount codes.

<img
  src="/images/discounts.png"
  alt="addproject"
  height="100"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

A popup will appear where you will be able to make the necessary changes

<img
  src="/images/editdiscount.png"
  alt="addproject"
  height="100"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

Pressing on the Update button will allow you to save the changes.
