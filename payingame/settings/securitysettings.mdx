- Password: If you want to renew your password, you can enter it Here.
- Confirm Password: Confirm your password in case you want to renew it.
- 2FA: If you want to enable 2FA, scan the QR code in an authenticator app like Google Authenticator. Once done, fill in the 6 numbers you see appearing on the authenticator. If all is fine, 2FA is enabled.
