- Username: You can change your username to your likings. By default it will be your email address with which you were subscribed with.
- Select Currency (dashboard): This allows you to change the default currency in which you want to see the reports.
- Upload your terms and conditions: Only a master admin can load the terms and conditions. These will be visible whenever a person wants to buy something. They will need to accept these before buying.
