Following items are needed in order to be able to access Payingame:

- Company name: This will be your company name
- VAT Number: As we need to invoice you for the fees, we will need to know where your company is located.
- Company Country: In addition to the VAT number, we request your country as well.
- Email: This is the email with which you are going to log on. It will also serve as a mean of communication (as an example for the confirmation of the subscription).
- Create Password: The password you are going to use to log on.
