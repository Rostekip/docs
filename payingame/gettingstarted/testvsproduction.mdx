---
title: "Test Vs Production"
description: "Payingame provides an easy way to switch from sandbox to production."
---

In the header, you will see a switch which is default on "test mode" once logged on.

<img
  src="/images/tstvsprod.png"
  alt="Subscribe"
  height="100"
  className="rounded-lg"
/>

Moving the slider will give you the possibility to move from sandbox to the production environment.
However, if you switch it to production, the system will keep track of your choice next time you log on.
